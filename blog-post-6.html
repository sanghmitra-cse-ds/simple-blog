Rainy Day Refactor

Mar 30, 2025 · 4 min read

The rain made the day feel like a single, long exhale. I opened the codebase that had been nagging at me for weeks and let the quiet slow my thinking. Instead of adding features, I took away lines: small extractions, clearer names, fewer surprises.

By evening the app behaved like it belonged to itself — simple and tidy. I made tea and watched the water rearrange the street. There was no parade of results, only the small satisfaction of cleaner work and a gentler mind.
